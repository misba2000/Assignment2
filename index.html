ENGINNERS DAY

The day is a homage to commemorate one of India's finest engineer's, Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya.
A small village called Muddenahalli in Karnataka,Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya was born on September 15, 1861. Due to his various contributions to the field and being a pioneer of education, he is also called as VM Sir.
